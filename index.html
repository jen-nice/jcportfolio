Hardware

Dance Dance Revolution

Verilog, Arduino(external inputs)

Digital Systems, U of T

This is an implementation of the popular game Dance Dance Revolution was built entirely in hardware. The ‘dance mat’ consisted of piezoelectric(pressure) sensors that converted foot pressure to voltage and an Arduino board helped to read in dance moves and passed them on to the main controller (Altera DE2 board). The project spanned three weeks and was a challenging but very rewarding experience with tons of laughs at the end, and yes, ‘Shake it off’ was the top song choice!
